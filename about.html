What is Saily?

Saily is a global eSIM service for travelers who want to stay connected on their trips. It offers hundreds of flexible travel data plans in over 150 countries, quick setup and 24/7 chat support. Get your travel data sorted with Saily — and enjoy smooth, safe, and reliable internet access while you’re away.

Created by Nord Security

Saily was created by Nord Security, the company behind one of the world’s most popular digital privacy tools, NordVPN. Believe it or not, eSIMs aren’t that different from VPNs. Both are designed to give people a reliable connection, especially away from home.

So, while a VPN protects your privacy on different networks, an eSIM gives you a secure and reliable internet connection when you travel.

Saily is owned by Nord Security, the global digital security and privacy brand behind NordVPN and NordPass.
