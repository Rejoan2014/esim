Does Saily provide bundle/regional plans?

Unfortunately, at the moment Saily doesn't provide regional or bundle plans at the moment, but you could create such a plan on your own, by purchasing different country's plans on the same eSIM profile.

Note: You can add other locations even after eSIM installation.

For example, to create a plan with several Europe region locations follow these steps:

1.Open the Saily app and select your eSIM (it might already have some plans purchased).

2.Select "Add data" .

3.Choose the location that you wish to add to your plan (in our case we add Lithuania, Denmark, and Sweden locations to our subscription)..

4.Choose the wanted data plan size from the available options and click "Continue". .

5.Complete the purchase process.

6.Finally, you will have all your plans located under the 1 eSIM profile.

7.Be sure to install your eSIM and activate your plans before the trip, you could activate all locations at once, or step by step during your trip, but we are aware that activation requires an internet connection.

In case you encounter any issues, don't hesitate to contact our 24/7 Customer Support Service by live chat or by contacting us at support@saily.com
